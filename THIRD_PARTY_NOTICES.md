# Third-Party Notices

xirasm-lib includes generated instruction/specification tables derived
from upstream specification data.

## RISC-V opcode data

The RISC-V encoder tables are generated from the `riscv-opcodes` project.
`riscv-opcodes` is distributed under the BSD-3-Clause license.

Upstream: https://github.com/riscv/riscv-opcodes

## SPIR-V grammar data

The SPIR-V encoder tables are generated from Khronos `SPIRV-Headers`
`include/spirv/unified1/spirv.core.grammar.json`.

The grammar/header material is distributed under the Khronos permissive
MIT-style license. Other non-code files in the upstream repository may use
separate documentation licenses.

Upstream: https://github.com/KhronosGroup/SPIRV-Headers
